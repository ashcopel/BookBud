Welcome to BookBud!

Description

This web application is designed to allow users to create book reviews, view
their inputted reviews, update their reviews, and/or delete a review.
Users can enjoy this application by keeping track of the books they read, browsing
the previous books they've read and/or sharing their reviews with other people.
